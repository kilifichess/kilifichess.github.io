The Kilifi County Chess Association was formally established in the year 2024 pursuant to the mandates stipulated under the Sports Act, which requires the constitution of county-level sporting associations. In observance of Article 10 of the Constitution of Kenya, this association is constituted to promote organized chess activities within Kilifi County, ensuring compliance with national governance structures and fostering the integrity, development, and regulation of the sport at the county level.

Our Mission

To promote the game of chess across Kilifi County by nurturing talent, encouraging strategic thinking, and fostering a vibrant chess community inclusive of players of all ages and skill levels.

Our Vision

To make Kilifi a leading chess hub in Kenya, recognized for developing world-class players, integrating chess into schools, and promoting the values of sportsmanship, critical thinking, and lifelong learning.

Chess in Schools

We collaborate with schools across Kilifi County to introduce chess clubs and offer chess training to young minds, promoting critical thinking and problem-solving skills.

Chess is actively played at Mudzini School and Sandpiper International School.

Home Chess Training

The Kilifi Chess Association has a team of dedicated, seasoned players who deliver home chess lessons for persons of any age.

Access online resources, tutorials, and training modules designed for players to improve their chess skills from the comfort of their homes.

Weekend Chess Practice

Join us every weekend for practice sessions open to all skill levels. Active practice happens on weekends at Pwani University, Kivukoni, and Mnarani, plus many other areas.

Dates, times, and venues are shared regularly, fostering community and friendly competition.
