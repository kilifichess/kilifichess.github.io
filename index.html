Our Mission

To promote the game of chess across Kilifi County by nurturing talent, encouraging strategic thinking, and fostering a vibrant chess community inclusive of players of all ages and skill levels.

Our Vision

To make Kilifi a leading chess hub in Kenya, recognized for developing world-class players, integrating chess into schools, and promoting the values of sportsmanship, critical thinking, and lifelong learning.
